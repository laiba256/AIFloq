How Automation Works

See Your
Workflow in Motion

AI Email Auto-Reply

Every incoming email is read, classified, and answered by a fine-tuned AI agent — matching your tone, referencing context, and escalating when needed.

  • Triggers instantly on any incoming IMAP or Gmail message
  • AI extracts sender intent and drafts a contextual reply
  • Routes complex queries to a human via Slack or Teams
  • Logs every interaction to your CRM automatically

Cold-to-Hot Lead Funnel

A conversational system that qualifies every new lead by asking the right questions — then routes them to booking, nurture, or disqualification automatically.

  • Captures leads from forms, ads, or direct messages
  • Asks budget, timeline, and service need via chat
  • Scores leads and routes them to the right pipeline
  • Books discovery calls on your calendar without you lifting a finger

WhatsApp AI Chatbot

An always-on AI agent with persistent memory that handles customer queries, collects information, and escalates intelligently — straight from WhatsApp.

  • Responds in seconds to any WhatsApp message
  • Remembers previous conversations for context
  • Handles FAQs, product info, and booking requests
  • Hands off to human agents with full transcript
